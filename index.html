My name is Matilde and I am a PhD student at Imperial College London.
My research mainly focuses on climate finance, household finance and sovereign debt.

Short bio

Born in a small town near Turin (Italy) in 1995, I graduated summa cum laude in both the B.A. in Business and Economics (CLABE) and the M.A. in Economics (LMEC) at the University of Bologna.

During my undergraduate studies I spent one semester at campus of the University of Bologna in Buenos Aires (Argentina).

While in my master I spent one semester at the University of California, Berkeley.

After that, I was an intern at the Monetary Policy Research Department in the European Central Bank.

Finally, I started my PhD at Imperial College London in September 2020.
